The Man Who Know Infinity

The Indian mathematician Srinivasa Ramanujan

Here is a brief history about him

  • 1887 - Born in Tamilnadu
  • 1892 - Ramanujan was enrolled at the local school.
  • 1903 - Ramanujan entered the Government College in Kumbakonam.
  • 1916 - Got degree from Tritiy college, Cambridge.
  • 1918 - He was elected "for his investigation in Elliptic functions and the Theory of Numbers." On 13 October 1918.
  • 1920 - At the age 32 he died
